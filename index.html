The Realm, Explained

The Wizard

You are a wizard. You have health (red), mana (blue), and experience (green). Walk over gold and health potions dropped by the enemies you unleashed terror on. Reaching enough XP grants a level-up where you choose one permanent boost — damage, fire rate, move speed, extra projectiles, crit, max HP, regen, lifesteal, or pickup range.

Damage & Crits

Your wand's base damage scales with your upgrade picks and gear.

Every hit can critically strike for bonus damage (shown as a big gold number that briefly flashes the screen). Floating numbers tell you what's happening: red = super effective, grey = resisted, gold! = critical hit, savage.

Elements - Don't worry too much yet

Every wand channels an element, and every enemy is made of one. Matching the right element multiplies your damage; the wrong one is resisted. The element also shapes how the spell looks, how fast it flies, and how big its blast is.

Triangle of power — Fire beats Nature · Frost breaks Storm · Storm wins over Frost · Nature settles Storm · Arcane pierces all (true damage, no weakness).

Enemies

Swarms grow per wave and hit harder the more you roam from the safe center. Wolves dash quick, slimes move all weird, sporelings & golems fling projectiles at you, gargoyles fly around. Gold-glowing Elites have triple health but reward gold, gear, potions and rare wands.

Key Spells

Wands evolve from a Wood Wand to the absolute animals that are Avatar and Nova. Wand mechanics: AoE blasts, chain lightning, orbiting saw-blades, homing and boomerang bolts, freeze fields, black-hole singularities, ricochet, railgun beams, cluster shards, and full 360° novas. Buy upgrades with gold, or find rare wands as drops.

The End

The world darkens with as you approach the Corrupted Core at the far edge. Cross the point of no return and face Zargoth, Warden of the Core — the final boss for now.
